General

How does Cura Home system work?

Cura Home system contain a camera and 2 kinds of smart sensor. User can connect the camera to WiFi network and connect smart sensors to camera. With this system, camera will send instant notification to your mobile phone if there is an event triggered. User can also check the live video or the video playback via mobile app.

Will I be notified when the bettery in my sensors is low?

Cura Home smart sensors supports low battery event, user will receive a notification when device battery is low. Or user can also check the LED of the sensors. Sensor's LED will blink once in every 3 seconds when device battery is low.
