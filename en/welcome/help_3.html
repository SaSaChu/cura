Technical Issue

Why I can't receive the the notification when a smart sensor triggered?

Smart sensors will report its status to camera and camera will upload those status to Cura server. However the camera might failed to receive the sensor status if the distance between sensor and camera is too far or there are too many obstacle. Please reduce the distance or the obstacles between sensor and camera and see if you can receive the notification.

Why app keeps pupping up "Please connect to device Wi-Fi network" when installing the camera by iOS app?

Please connect your mobile phone to camera's WiFi network after you input the WiFi password which you want camera to connect. The WiFi name is Cura-Home-XXXXXXXXXX, and no password required. And then go back to the app and click "Continue" to move forward.

Why I can't pair the correct sensor that I want to use?

In the sensor pairing mode, camera will pair the sensor automatically when there is a sensor trigger, so please make sure you have triggered the correct sensor when you are pairing the smart sensor with your camera.
