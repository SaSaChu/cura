Installation

How to install the camera and smart sensors?

Cura Home camera can be separate to base and body by screwing the camera body. You can fix the base at the surface you want to install the camera then scew camera body back to the base.

For the smart sensors, you can fix the PIR bracket at the surface you want to install the PIR sensor then assamble the bracket and the sensor. For the door/window sensor, you can fix the sensor on the door or window by using double-side tape or screws.

Can I install camera or smart sensors in outdoor environment?

Cura Home is not for ourdoor environment, the camera and smart sensors might damaged by rain or dust.

Does Cura Home system support multiple user at the same time?

Cura Home system will be bind with specific account, but user can still login same account via different devices at the same time, or share their camera video to other account.
